The History of Harvest Clicks

Websites That Get Results

We build custom websites for contractors, tradespeople, and other businesses, that actually bring in leads. No complicated dashboards, no tech headaches — just a professional site that shows up on Google, looks great on phones, and turns visitors into paying customers. We know what your customers are looking for when they need a roofer, plumber, electrician, or builder, and we design sites that make them want to call you first.

Why I Started This Company

My name is Andrew Beiler, here’s why I got into this business. My background is in the construction industry; I've done everything from roofing and kitchen remodels to building horse barns and storage sheds. I enjoyed the work, but I never felt like I'd found my true career. That changed after my son was born and I wanted a career that allowed me to be home more. I discovered I had a passion for computer programming and web development, and suddenly everything clicked. This business is where my two worlds came together. Now I get to combine my hands-on experience in the trades with my passion for technology to help other business owners like the ones I used to work alongside succeed.

What Sets Us Apart

Because of my background, I know how to build a website that actually works as a tool for your business. You don't need another cluttered site with a confusing design that drives customers away instead of bringing them in. That's why I partner with talented designers and copywriters who are great at marketing in your industry. This approach gives you the best of both worlds, my real-world industry knowledge combined with professional marketing expertise that converts visitors into customers.

Our Simple Process

  • Consultation

    The first step is to get to know you, and your business goals. This step includes a zoom call or an in-person consultation (depending on your location), and filling out a questionnaire to fill in more details.

  • Design

    Our design team will get to work to make your dream website come to life. By combining your unique insights with their professional expertise, they create a beautiful website designed to achieve your goals.

  • Launch & Beyond

    We’ll wrap things up with a personal video call to tour your new website together. This however is not the end of our partnership with your business — instead it’s just the beginning. We’re here for any updates and support that you need!
